About BJJ

Brazilian Jiujitsu or Brasileiro is a self defence martial art and combat sport based on grappling, ground fighting and submission holds. It derives from Jiujitsu and Judo from Japan.

Effectivenss

BJJ is incredibly effective in fighting conditions or when protecting yourself. Enables the person to take control of the oppeonent and finish the fight without hurting the other person as much.

History

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu was developed in the 1920s by Brazilian brothers Carlos, Oswaldo, Gastão Jr., George and Hélio Gracie.
Created in 1917.

Belts

In BJJ, there are levels in the martial art. Starting from white, blue, purple, brown, black, coral and red belt.It takes time to move on to the next belt and a minimum of two years for each belt before moving up a belt.

Benefits

Students of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu frequently report feeling a deeper understanding of their body, mind, and spirit by simply training and practicing the martial art. Furthermore, BJJ helps to identify physical and mental strengths, limitations, opportunities and also enables an improved state of peacefulness.

Groundwork In MMA

For individuals that trains in mixed martial arts, having BJJ under their belt as a skill set will help them in their game. MMA is a mixture of everything and the more they know, more advantage in a fight.
