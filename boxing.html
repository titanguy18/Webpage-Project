Boxing


| Traditional Boxing |

Starting Out

It is a sport where you and your opponent will wear regulation gloves and other gear such as headgear depending the level of competition. Basic gears are hand wraps, mouthguards and basically two opponents thorw punches at each other fro a certain amount of rounds. There could be 8 rounds until 12 rounds of a boxing match.

Strength and Conditioning

Boxers training has come along way since few decades ago. But most of the training mostly consist of strength based exercises which are bodyweight exercises. It would consist of push-ups, sit-ups, crunches, jump-ropes and burpees.

Training Plan

To our members who are a complete novice in combat sport in general, we will train them up step by step. Getting the basics right which is the foundation of everything. Have the fitness to be on point. From then on we will progress to another level and all depends on the member and their capability.

Trainers

Depending on the wether it is a group or individual class, that will differ on the type of trainer that will be incharge. Doing so will suit the level of the members in the class to keep up and teach what a person is lacking.

Benefits

Overall, by learning how to box, you are already able to defend yourself in general and in most situations. Other than that you will get fitter, improved stamina and overall health will be better than before.
