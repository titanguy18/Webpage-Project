| More About Us! |

What we offer to out members?

We have a variety of classes for you to join. Doesn't matter if you want to learn to defend yourself in public, or wanting to find a new hobby, we got you covered. We have classes everyday and you can come on which day you seem fit depending on your schedule.

Our facilities

Our gym is equipped with everything from combat training equipement, strength and cardio equipement. We have a section just for working out with cardio machines, weights and members can come and use them anytime they want.

Trainers

All our trainers are certified and are real professionals. Some of them are former or current fighters in their field and have more than a decade of experience. They are there to help you and will train each member according to their fitness level and experience.

| Benefits of Joining Us |

Self Defence

Learn to protect yourself in today's world where crime rate is not getting any lower. Being able to defend yourself will be a skillset that will benefit your family, friends and yourself.

Be Active and Get Healthier

Besides learning to defend yourself, you also become fitter as you go when joining our gym. The training involves alot cardio and helps build endurance. A chance to meet people and make more friends, increase your social circle.

Opportunity to Present Us

For the individuals that would want to pursue to be a fighter or is currently one, we can offer you the best coaches. Also with that training plans, and fights for you to compete in and help you get more sponsors.


| Our Goals & Vision |

"Our objectives as a gym is to provide our members the highest quality of education in terms of combat sport and how to defend themselves. We want everyone who took part in our classes to be able to really learn and implement what we thought them in the real world.

To learn the honour and integrity that comes with martial arts and the meaning of sportsmanship. Not to miss use the skills they have obtained from us and use it to help someone rather than inflicting pain and fear on to them."
